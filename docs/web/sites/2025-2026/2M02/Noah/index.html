Different goth fashion styles and their origins

Goth is a clothing subgenre derived from late 1970s-1980s post-punk scenes. In mainstream media today, it's often presented as all fishnets, tight leather, and a sexualized vibe. Historically, the look was more about mood, artistic darkness, and identity experimentation. Dramatic makeup, layered clothing, and androgynous silhouettes were used to challenge norms, not to be “sexy.” The modern emphasis on sexuality is largely commercialization. See more here.
