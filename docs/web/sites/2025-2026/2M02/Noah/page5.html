地図

Cyber goth is an industrial-adjacent style with heavy rave influence. Think neon accents on a black base, synthetic materials, goggles, tubes/dreads, and a high-contrast, almost sci-fi silhouette. It's loud on purpose.

Tip: if everything is neon, nothing is neon—keep a dark base and pick 1-2 accent colors.
