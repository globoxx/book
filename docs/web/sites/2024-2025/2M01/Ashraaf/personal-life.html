Lewis Hamilton's Personal Life

Beyond the racetrack, Lewis Hamilton is a multifaceted individual with passions and commitments that extend far beyond motorsport.

Passion for Environmental Sustainability

Hamilton is an outspoken advocate for environmental awareness. He adopted a vegan lifestyle in 2017 and promotes sustainable living.

Musical Talents

A music enthusiast, Lewis has contributed to music projects under the pseudonym "XNDA" and enjoys expressing his creativity in the studio.

Fashion Icon

Known for his unique and bold fashion sense, Lewis regularly collaborates with major fashion brands and makes statements on the red carpet.

Philanthropy

Through the Hamilton Commission and his charitable efforts, he aims to increase diversity in motorsport and support underrepresented communities.
