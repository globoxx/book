地図

Romantic goth draws inspiration from Victorian and medieval aesthetics: elegance, drama, and dark romanticism. You'll see lace, velvet, chiffon, high collars, corsets, long skirts, ruffled sleeves, and intricate jewelry in deep colors like black, burgundy, and plum. Compared to punk-leaning goth, it's softer and more historical in silhouette.

Notes
Romantic goth leans historical: lace, velvet, corsetry, and drama.
