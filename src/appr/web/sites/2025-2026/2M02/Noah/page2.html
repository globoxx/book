地図

Pastel goth blends traditionally dark, edgy goth aesthetics with soft, whimsical pastel colors. It mixes contrasts—black leather, fishnets, occult or punk-inspired motifs—paired with lavender hair, bubble-gum pink skirts, and cute accessories. The tension between “creepy” and “cute” is the point.

Notes
Pastel goth is built on contrast: cute + dark on purpose.
